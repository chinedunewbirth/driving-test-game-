1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how UK Driving Test Simulator ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. All data generated by your use of the app (quiz scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device only using Android's SharedPreferences storage.

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We have no servers, databases, or back-end systems that receive or store your data. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or social media platforms.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 17 and above (the minimum driving age in the UK). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive device permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.).

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
